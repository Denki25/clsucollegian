Why Join Us?

The CLSU Collegian offers students a unique opportunity to develop their writing, editing, and multimedia skills while contributing to the campus community. As a member of our team, you'll gain valuable experience in journalism, learn to navigate complex issues, and make a real impact on student life.

Skill Development

Learn professional writing, editing, photography, and digital media skills.

Networking

Connect with like-minded students, faculty, and industry professionals.

Impact

Make a difference in your campus community through meaningful storytelling.

Experience

Gain practical experience that will enhance your resume and future career.
