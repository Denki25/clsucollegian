Our Story

The CLSU Collegian is the official student publication of Central Luzon State University, serving as the primary voice for the student body since its establishment in 1965. As a cornerstone of campus journalism, we are committed to delivering accurate, unbiased, and compelling content that informs, engages, and empowers our readers.

Our publication covers a wide range of topics including campus news, student life, academic developments, sports, cultural events, and issues affecting the broader university community. We strive to maintain the highest standards of journalistic integrity while fostering critical thinking and open dialogue among students.

🎯

To provide comprehensive, accurate, and timely information about university affairs, promote student welfare, and serve as a platform for diverse voices and perspectives within the CLSU community.
